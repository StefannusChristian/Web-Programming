Our History

Stefannus Caleb, the company's founder, began by repairing broken instruments in 1783. He built his original music instruments shortly after that. We defined the Hilltone Philosophy as the conceptual foundation that drives company management within the Yamaha Group, more than 125 years after our founding in 1783. In addition, we launched our new brand promise " When Words Leave Off, Music Begins" in 2022 to capture that particular moment when our customers' hearts begin to quake.
